Selected work

Standardizing customer support tooling across LatAm — Superbid

Sep 2021 – Aug 2023

Support overflow across Superbid's LatAm markets was landing on first-line teams with no structured workflow behind it — on the order of 60 tickets a month were spilling over. I coordinated a cross-functional customer-found-defects tracking initiative, connecting Legal, Sales, Operations, Engineering, and Product across Brazil and the wider LatAm footprint onto one system, and drove standardized help-desk tooling and processes across countries.

Overflow dropped by roughly 54%, consistent across three separate points in my own record-keeping.

Removing manual data-onboarding drag — Round Pegs / Superbid

Jul 2020 – Aug 2021

Every time a large client onboarded, Superbid's digitization function hit a wall: heterogeneous file formats had to be processed by hand, in bursts, slowing everything down. I led a small multifunctional squad to replace that manual step with standardized file-input formats and scripts that ingested client source files directly into the system, cutting over from legacy tooling to distributed microservices.

Cycle time in the digitization function improved by roughly 21% — the most consistently corroborated metric in my record of this period, and the work that led directly to a move into a Head of Product role.

Rebuilding a legacy internal tool company-wide — Avalara

2024 – 2026

At Avalara, an internal platform tool relied on daily by 50+ people company-wide was still running on an aging, tightly-coupled interface. Over roughly two years — the full React rebuild itself starting in early 2026 — I directed the redesign end-to-end: product direction, cross-team coordination, and hands-on input into the solution design, while engineering migrated the tool onto a GraphQL abstraction layer and rebuilt the interface in React.

Rolled out to the entire user base as of mid-2026, with a clean cutover.
