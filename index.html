01Avalara · 2024 – 2026

Rebuilding a legacy internal tool company-wide

More than 80 people open the same internal tool every day to turn jurisdiction tax forms into forms the platform can fill and file correctly. It was still running on an aging, tightly coupled interface, and it could not simply stop while we replaced it.

Over about two years I directed the rebuild end to end, owning product direction, cross-team coordination, and hands-on input into the solution design, while engineering moved the tool onto a GraphQL abstraction layer and rebuilt the interface in React.

Rolled out to the entire 80+ user base by mid-2026, with a clean cutover.

02Superbid · 2021 – 2023

Standardizing customer support tooling across LatAm

Every month, around 60 support tickets spilled past the first line with nowhere structured to go. Five departments touched them, none owned them, and every country we operated in handled them differently.

I coordinated the customer-found-defects initiative that pulled Legal, Sales, Operations, Engineering, and Product onto a single system, and drove standardized help-desk tooling across Brazil and the rest of LatAm.

Overflow dropped by roughly 54%, consistent across three separate points in my records.

03Round Pegs / Superbid · 2020 – 2021

Removing manual data-onboarding drag

The biggest clients created the worst spikes: every large onboarding arrived as heterogeneous files someone processed by hand, in bursts. The team had learned to live with it.

I led a small multifunctional squad that replaced the manual step with standardized file-input formats and scripts feeding client source files straight into the system, moving us off legacy tooling and onto distributed microservices.

Cycle time improved by roughly 21%; the work led directly to a Head of Product role.
