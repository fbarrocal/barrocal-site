Selected work

Rebuilding a legacy internal tool company-wide

Avalara  ·  2024 – 2026

At Avalara, an internal platform tool that 80+ people rely on daily was still running on an aging, tightly-coupled interface. Over about two years, with the full React rebuild starting in early 2026, I directed the redesign from end to end. I owned product direction, cross-team coordination, and hands-on input into the solution design, while engineering migrated the tool onto a GraphQL abstraction layer and rebuilt the interface in React.

It rolled out to the entire user base by mid-2026, with a clean cutover.

Standardizing customer support tooling across LatAm

Superbid  ·  Sep 2021 – Aug 2023

Support overflow across Superbid's LatAm markets kept landing on first-line teams that had no structured workflow to absorb it. Around 60 tickets a month were spilling over. I coordinated a cross-functional customer-found-defects tracking initiative that connected Legal, Sales, Operations, Engineering, and Product across Brazil and the wider LatAm region onto a single system, and I drove standardized help-desk tooling and processes across the countries we operated in.

Overflow dropped by roughly 54%, a figure that held consistent across three separate points in my own records.

Removing manual data-onboarding drag

Round Pegs / Superbid  ·  Jul 2020 – Aug 2021

Every time a large client onboarded, Superbid's digitization function hit a wall. Heterogeneous file formats had to be processed by hand, in bursts, and that slowed everything down. I led a small multifunctional squad that replaced the manual step with standardized file-input formats and scripts that fed client source files straight into the system, moving us off legacy tooling and onto distributed microservices.

Cycle time in the digitization function improved by roughly 21%. It's the most consistently corroborated metric in my records from that period, and the work led directly to a move into a Head of Product role.
